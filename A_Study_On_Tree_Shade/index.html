Shade score; the purpose of this self-made shade score is because I wanted to rank the families of trees in the town of Lawrence for further analysis in how this town layout is in terms of getting the best shade. This might come in handy when deciding which trees should be planted in the future. Shade score = (count) x (family) /10.

Thus, after one looks at these visualizations, it is apparent that the town of Lawrence MA has a wide variety of trees that give excellent amount of shade to the community. Though the number of tree plants have decreased, if there is a case where more trees are needed for shade or other purposes, there are many ways to choose a tree, such as diameter and shade score. Shade score is a great way to choose due to the balance between commonality and diameter, which takes a fair look on price and width of the tree for shade.
